Our Vision

Our main goal is to propose a multidisciplinary and cost effective approach to control and prevention of Tuberculosis. Determination of standards and maintenance of quality diagnosis and treatment of Tuberculosis is of prime importance. We put forward a platform to report the results of these diagnosis by doctors and other medical practitioners regarding Tuberculosis. Ultimately, our main aim is to eradicate Tuberculosis.
